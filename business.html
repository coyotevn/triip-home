BE THE CHANGE

By joining Triip ecosystem, you’re sharing our same mission of tackling an accelerating ecological crisis and driving tourism industry toward environmental sustainability. It’s a sole purpose of Triip’s existence so we’re offering all who sign the Triip’s Pledge an amount of token as a presence of appreciation. To realize our mission, Triip is contributing 1% of each booking conducted in Triip ecosystem to a Sustainable Fund that we’re using to advance a series of projects for environmental impact, including plastic waste reduction program SaveYourOcean.com.
